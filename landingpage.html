Wild buffalo

Chhattisgarh's last purebred wild buffalo, 21-year-old 'Chhotu' fights for survial. Raipur: Despite millions spent on conservation efforts, only one purebred wild buffalo, the state animal of Chhattisgarh, remains within the boundaries of Udanti Sitanadi Tiger Reserve in the Gariaband district of the state.
